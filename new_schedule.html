Exercise Plan

Goal Type Days per Week Workout Split
Fat loss and muscle gain Home-based indoor exercises 6
  • Day 1: HIIT
    • Burpees - 3 sets of 10-15 reps
    • High Knees - 3 sets of 30 seconds
    • Mountain Climbers - 3 sets of 30 seconds
    • Jumping Jacks - 3 sets of 30 seconds
  • Day 2: Lower Body
    • Squats - 3 sets of 12-15 reps
    • Lunges - 3 sets of 10-12 reps per leg
    • Glute Bridges - 3 sets of 12-15 reps
    • Calf Raises - 3 sets of 15-20 reps
  • Day 3: Upper Body
    • Push-Ups - 3 sets of 8-12 reps
    • Tricep Dips (using a chair) - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
    • Plank to Shoulder Taps - 3 sets of 10 taps per side
    • Side Lateral Raises (using water bottles) - 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Day 4: Core
    • Plank - 3 sets of 30-60 seconds
    • Bicycle Crunches - 3 sets of 15-20 reps per side
    • Russian Twists - 3 sets of 15-20 reps per side
    • Leg Raises - 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Day 5: Full Body
    • Burpees - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
    • Squat Jumps - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
    • Push-Ups - 3 sets of 8-12 reps
    • Plank - 3 sets of 30-60 seconds
  • Day 6: Active Recovery
    • Gentle Yoga for relaxation and stretching

Yoga Poses

Pose Duration Description
Child's Pose 1-2 minutes A resting pose that stretches the back and hips.
Cat-Cow Pose 1-2 minutes Alternating between arching and rounding the back to relieve tension.
Downward-Facing Dog 1-2 minutes A full-body stretch that focuses on the back and hamstrings.
Seated Forward Bend 1-2 minutes Stretching the back and hamstrings while seated.
Bridge Pose 1-2 minutes Strengthens the back and stretches the chest and hips.
