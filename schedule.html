Yoga Poses

Pose Description
Cat-Cow Stretch On hands and knees, alternate arching and rounding your back.
Child's Pose Seated on your heels, stretch forward with arms extended, resting your forehead on the ground.
Downward Dog From hands and knees, lift hips to form an inverted V shape.
Cobra Pose Lie on your stomach, place hands under shoulders, and lift your chest while keeping hips on the ground.
Seated Forward Bend Sitting with legs extended, reach forward towards your toes, keeping back straight.
Bridge Pose Lie on your back, bend knees, and lift your hips while pressing into your feet.
